# Load Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 8, 2026**

---

## 1. Who We Are

Load is developed and operated by Kaylee Hirsch, an individual developer based in Oregon, United States ("we," "us," or "our"). References to "we," "us," or "our" include any legal entity through which Load may be operated in the future.

**Privacy contact:** privacy@techyshishy.com

We will respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.

---

## 2. Scope of This Policy

This policy describes how we collect and use personal data when you use Load's online features.

**This policy does not cover single-player save data.** When you play Load without an account, your game save is stored exclusively on your own device — in your browser's local storage or your device's local storage. That data is never transmitted to or stored on our servers. We have no access to it.

---

## 3. Data We Collect

What we collect depends entirely on how you use Load.

### If you play single-player without an account

We collect no personal data.

### If you create an account to access multiplayer features

**Account data** (received from your identity provider at sign-in):

- A unique account identifier issued by your identity provider
- Your display name
- Your avatar image URL

We do not request or receive your email address. We do not request access to your contacts, calendar, location, or any other personal data beyond the three items listed above.

**Gameplay data:**

- Your game saves, match history, and in-game progress, linked to your account identifier

**Technical connection data:**

- Your IP address, collected automatically as a byproduct of your device connecting to our servers
- Standard client metadata (browser or app version, operating system, device type) included automatically in HTTP requests

---

## 4. How We Use Your Data

| Data | Purpose | Legal basis under GDPR |
|---|---|---|
| Account identifier, display name, avatar | Identifying your account across sessions; displaying your name and avatar to other players | Performance of contract |
| Gameplay data | Saving your progress; enabling multiplayer features | Performance of contract |
| IP address, client metadata | Security, abuse prevention, infrastructure reliability | Legitimate interests |

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral profiling, or sale to third parties. We do not make automated decisions about you that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

## 5. Identity Providers

You sign in to Load using a third-party identity provider. We currently support:

- **Google** — [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 6. Third-Party Services

### Amazon Web Services (AWS)

Our server infrastructure runs on AWS. Your data is stored exclusively in the AWS region that corresponds to where you play (see Section 7). We have accepted AWS's Data Processing Agreement; AWS processes your data only on our instructions.
[AWS Privacy Notice](https://aws.amazon.com/privacy/)

### Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how the game is used and to identify technical issues. Google Analytics may collect data such as session duration, in-game events, crash reports, and general device and platform information. This data is associated with a randomly assigned analytics identifier — it is not linked to your Load account identifier or your identity provider profile.

You can opt out of Google Analytics collection at any time:

- Enable the "Do Not Track" signal in your browser or app settings
- Use the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) (web)

[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Purchase Platforms

Load is available for purchase through Google Play and itch.io. Payments are processed entirely by those platforms under their own privacy policies. We do not process payment card data and do not receive your financial information. We receive only a confirmation that a purchase was completed, used solely to grant you access to the game.

- [Google Play Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [itch.io Privacy Policy](https://itch.io/docs/legal/privacy-policy)

### Future Services

We may add additional infrastructure, analytics, or crash-reporting services in the future. We will update this policy before activating any new service that processes your personal data, and we will notify you of material changes as described in Section 13.

## 7. Where Your Data Is Stored

We deliberately design our infrastructure so that your data stays in the region where you play:

| Your play region | Where your data is stored |
|---|---|
| EU / EEA | EU-region AWS infrastructure. Your data does not leave the EU/EEA. |
| United States | US-region AWS infrastructure |
| Other regions | The AWS region geographically closest to your play region |

**A transfer of EU/EEA player data to a non-EU/EEA server is not an intended behavior — it is a defect.** We treat any such cross-region transfer as a data breach subject to our incident response process.

For EU/EEA players, data processing is governed by AWS's Data Processing Agreement and Standard Contractual Clauses, which provide protections equivalent to EU data protection law.

---

## 8. How Long We Keep Your Data

| Data type | Retention |
|---|---|
| Account data and gameplay data | Kept while your account is active |
| Account data and gameplay data after account deletion | Removed from our active database immediately. Encrypted backup copies are purged within 90 days of deletion. |
| Technical connection logs (IP address, client metadata) | Retained for 30 days |
| Google Analytics data | Governed by Google's retention settings; we configure the minimum retention period available |

---

## 9. Deleting Your Account

You can delete your account at any time from within the game's settings. When you do:

- Your account data and gameplay data are deleted from our active database immediately.
- Encrypted backup copies that may contain your data are purged within 90 days.
- Your single-player local save is not affected — it stays on your device and is not within our control.

You can also request deletion by emailing privacy@techyshishy.com. We will confirm when deletion is complete.

---

## 10. Children's Privacy

Load is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under 13. Account creation requires you to confirm that you are at least 13 years of age.

If we learn that an account was created by a user under 13, we will promptly delete that account and all associated data.

If you believe a child under 13 has created a Load account, please contact us at privacy@techyshishy.com.

## 11. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights. To exercise any of them, email privacy@techyshishy.com.

**Right to access.** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

**Right to correction.** Request that we correct inaccurate data. Note that your display name and avatar are controlled by your identity provider (Google, GitHub, or Apple) — to change them, update them there.

**Right to deletion.** Delete your account and all associated data (see Section 9).

**Right to data portability.** Request your gameplay data in a machine-readable format.

**Right to restrict processing.** Ask us to limit how we use your data while a dispute is resolved.

**Right to object.** Object to processing that is based on our legitimate interests.

**No automated decision-making.** We do not use your data for automated profiling or decisions that significantly affect you. There is nothing to opt out of.

**EU/EEA residents.** In addition to the above, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the data protection supervisory authority in your member state.

**California residents (CCPA/CPRA).** You have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or cross-context behavioral advertising.

We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

## 12. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including TLS encryption in transit and encryption at rest for stored data. Access to personal data is restricted to what is necessary to operate the service.

No system is perfectly secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to privacy@techyshishy.com.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

When we update this policy, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top. For material changes — changes that affect what data we collect, how we use it, or who we share it with — we will provide in-game notice before the change takes effect. For EU/EEA residents, we will notify you directly before any change that affects your rights or our lawful basis for processing.

Continuing to use Load's multiplayer features after a policy change takes effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
